About Us

iAfrica is a not-for-profit organization registered under the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 165321) whose primary mission is to reduce the number of out-of-school children in Nigeria by providing them with basic literacy education, particularly those in underserved communities across Nigeria.

Our Mission

Providing wholesome, qualitative reading and writing learning experiences to every African child.

Vision

To inspire a tradition for lifelong learning by providing basic literacy skills to every underserved African child.
