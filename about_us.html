About Us

iAfrica is a not-for-profit organisation registered under the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 165321) whose primary mission is to reduce the number of out-of-school children in Nigeria by providing basic literacy education to children in underserved children across Nigeria.

Our Mission

Chart the directions of customised policies that would bring Out-Of-School Children (OOSC) back to school Take “school” to as many OOSC as possible by 2030 Provide access to 21st century online resources to remote communities Provide remote learning to children in communities ravaged by insurgency Enhance teaching and learning processes for both the teachers and children

Important Facts

One in every five of the world's out-of-school children is in Nigeria (UNICEF). Nigeria accounts for 45% of all out-of-school children in West Africa, with over 10 million out-of-school children (Malala.org). All children, no matter where they live or what their circumstances, have the right to quality education (UNICEF).
